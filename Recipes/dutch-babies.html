Dutch Babies

Dutch Babies are a light, fluffy breakfast dish to be served with warm maple syrup and topped with powdered sugar or fruit. This recipe should take around half an hour to prepare and is great for 3-5 people.

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  1. Put cast-iron skillet in oven and preheat to 475 degrees farenheit
  2. Whisk eggs in bowl. Add in milk while mixing. Then add flour, nutmeg, and salt.
  3. Remove skillet from oven and butter the pan. Pour in ingredients. Reduce heat to 425 degrees farenheit and place skillet back into oven.
  4. Cook until golden brown (approximately 12 minutes).
  5. Remove from oven and immediately add powdered sugar.
  6. Serve hot with maple syrup and/or fruit.
