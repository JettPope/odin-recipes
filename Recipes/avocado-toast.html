Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs is a nutritious and satisfying breakfast option. This recipe combines the creamy texture of avocado with perfectly poached eggs on crispy toast. It's quick to make and packed with healthy fats and protein.

  • Whole Grain Bread
  • Ripe Avocados
  • Poached Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Red Pepper Flakes (optional)
  • Chopped Fresh Cilantro
  1. Toast slices of whole grain bread until golden and crispy.
  2. Mash ripe avocados and spread them evenly over the toasted bread.
  3. Top each slice with a perfectly poached egg.
  4. Season with salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired.
  5. Sprinkle chopped fresh cilantro on top for added freshness.
  6. Enjoy your nutritious and delicious avocado toast with poached eggs!
