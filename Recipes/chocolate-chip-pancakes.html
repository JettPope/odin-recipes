Chocolate Chip Pancakes

Chocolate Chip Pancakes are a delightful breakfast treat, perfect for chocolate lovers. Serve these fluffy pancakes with a drizzle of chocolate syrup and a dollop of whipped cream. This recipe yields approximately 10 pancakes and takes about 20 minutes to prepare.

  • All-purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Butter
  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix milk and eggs. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  3. Gently fold in chocolate chips into the batter.
  4. Heat a griddle or skillet over medium heat and melt a little butter.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup portions of batter onto the griddle. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with chocolate syrup and whipped cream.
